# SafeExif Ethics and Principles

SafeExif is designed with a foundational philosophy that guides every decision in development, design, and future expansion.

## 1. Agency and User Control
- All actions are initiated and controlled by the user
- Users decide which files to inspect and scrub
- The app does not assume the role of protector; it provides knowledge and tools

## 2. Privacy and Local-First Processing
- No file or metadata is uploaded to any server
- No analytics or telemetry are collected
- All processing occurs entirely on the user’s device

## 3. Transparency and Honesty
- Reports are human-readable and factual
- No scores, alarmist warnings, or fear-based messaging
- Scrubbing actions are explicit, reversible, and verifiable

## 4. Ethical Growth
- Future tools under the SafeExif / Keep Model umbrella will follow the same principles
- Subscription-based or hidden-fee models are not aligned with the philosophy
- Security and metadata literacy take precedence over monetization or hype

## 5. Limitations
- SafeExif does not guarantee protection against advanced spyware or nation-state attacks
- The app empowers informed decisions but cannot prevent all forms of tracking
- Users remain responsible for how files are shared or stored after scrubbing
